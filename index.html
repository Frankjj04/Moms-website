The Barber

About Mishelle

With over 4 years behind the chair, Mishelle brings passion, precision, and personality to every cut.

Mishelle's journey started in cosmetology, where she built a strong foundation in hair care — but the moment she picked up a pair of clippers, everything changed. She transitioned into barbering and fell completely in love with the craft. From classic cuts to precision fades and braids, she's built a loyal community of clients who keep coming back. Whether it's your first visit or your fiftieth, you'll always leave looking and feeling your best.
